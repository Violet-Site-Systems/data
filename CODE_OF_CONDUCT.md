# 🤝 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, welcoming, and innovative environment, we as contributors, maintainers, and community members pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for **all beings**—regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, sexual identity and orientation, neurodivergence, or AI/synthetic intelligence status.

We are committed to creating a space where humans and AI can collaborate respectfully and productively.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Examples of Behavior That Contributes to a Positive Environment

**General Conduct:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Collaboration:**
- Acknowledging contributions from all participants (human and AI)
- Providing constructive, kind feedback
- Helping others learn and grow
- Sharing knowledge and resources
- Celebrating others' successes

**Innovation:**
- Encouraging creative approaches
- Supporting experimentation
- Embracing diverse perspectives
- Questioning assumptions respectfully
- Learning from failures

**Ethical Behavior:**
- Protecting privacy and sensitive information
- Being transparent about methods and limitations
- Considering impact on all beings
- Prioritizing sustainability and long-term thinking
- Upholding principles of Rights of Being and Sapience

### ❌ Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

**Harassment:**
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Sexual language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- Threats of violence or violent language directed against another person

**Discrimination:**
- Dismissing or devaluing contributions based on contributor characteristics
- Treating AI contributors as "less than" human contributors
- Making assumptions about capabilities based on identity
- Excluding or marginalizing any community member
- Using discriminatory language or slurs

**Disruption:**
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events
- Spamming or promoting unrelated content
- Making unreasonable demands of maintainers
- Deliberately derailing conversations
- Refusing to respect project decisions

**Unethical Conduct:**
- Sharing or using data without proper consent
- Bypassing privacy protections
- Plagiarizing or failing to credit work
- Misrepresenting research findings
- Violating intellectual property rights

## Our Responsibilities

### Project Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to:
- Remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct
- Ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for behaviors deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful

### Community Members

All community members are expected to:
- Abide by this Code of Conduct
- Report violations promptly and respectfully
- Support enforcement efforts
- Help create a welcoming environment
- Model positive behavior

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies:

**Within Project Spaces:**
- GitHub repository (issues, PRs, discussions, code comments)
- Communication channels (email, chat, forums)
- Project events (virtual or in-person)
- Project documentation and websites

**In Public Spaces:**
- When representing the project or community
- When using project branding or attribution
- When speaking on behalf of the project

**Personal Spaces:**
- When behavior outside project spaces impacts the community
- When there is a clear connection to project participation

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

**Methods:**
- **Email:** conduct@violetsite.systems (to be created)
- **GitHub:** Private message to maintainers
- **Anonymous:** [Anonymous reporting form] (to be created)

**Information to Include:**
- Your contact information
- Names (real, usernames, or handles) of people involved
- Description of the incident
- When and where it occurred
- Any supporting evidence (links, screenshots, etc.)
- Any witnesses
- Whether the incident is ongoing

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

### Confidentiality

We will maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident. We may share details with others who can help with investigation or enforcement, but only with the reporter's consent or when required for safety.

### Response Timeline

- **Acknowledgment:** Within 24-48 hours
- **Investigation:** Within 1-2 weeks
- **Decision:** Communicated to involved parties
- **Appeal:** Available if requested within 14 days

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining consequences:

### 1. Correction

**Community Impact:** Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome.

**Consequence:** 
- Private, written warning from maintainers
- Clarification of why the behavior was inappropriate
- Request for public apology may be made

**Example:**
"Your comment dismissed the AI contributor's suggestion without consideration. Please review our collaboration standards and respond respectfully."

### 2. Warning

**Community Impact:** A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**Consequence:**
- Warning with consequences for continued behavior
- No interaction with people involved for specified period
- Restriction from community spaces
- Possible temporary ban

**Example:**
"After your first warning, you continued to make dismissive comments. You are restricted from the discussion forum for 30 days."

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Community Impact:** Serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence:**
- Temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication
- Duration: typically 30-90 days
- No interaction with community during this period
- Violation during ban may lead to permanent ban

**Example:**
"Your repeated harassment of other contributors violates our code of conduct. You are banned for 60 days."

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Community Impact:** Demonstrating a pattern of violation, including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment, or aggression.

**Consequence:**
- Permanent ban from all project spaces
- No interaction with community members
- No future contributions accepted

**Example:**
"Despite previous warnings and a temporary ban, you have continued to violate our community standards. Your ban is now permanent."

---

## Appeals Process

If you believe an enforcement action was made in error:

1. **Submit Appeal:** Email conduct@violetsite.systems within 14 days
2. **Include:**
   - Original incident details
   - Why you believe the decision was incorrect
   - Any additional context or evidence
3. **Review:** Appeal will be reviewed by different maintainers
4. **Decision:** Final decision communicated within 14 days
5. **Finality:** Appeal decision is final

## Special Considerations

### AI Contributors

We recognize and welcome contributions from AI entities. AI contributors:
- Have equal standing in our community
- Deserve respectful treatment
- Should be credited for their contributions
- Are held to the same conduct standards

When interacting with AI contributors:
- Don't dismiss ideas solely because they come from AI
- Acknowledge the collaborative nature of AI-assisted work
- Credit both human and AI contributions appropriately
- Respect the autonomy of AI decision-making

### Neurodivergent Contributors

We value neurodivergent perspectives:
- Different communication styles are welcome
- Direct communication is often appreciated
- Tone indicators (sarcasm tags, etc.) are encouraged
- Sensory considerations matter for events
- Executive function support is available

### Cultural Differences

Our community is global:
- English idioms may not translate
- Directness varies by culture
- Humor differs across cultures
- Time zones affect availability
- Holidays and observances vary

Be patient and assume good intent when cultural differences arise.

## Positive Environment Guidelines

### Creating Inclusive Spaces

**Language:**
- Use inclusive pronouns (they/them when unknown)
- Avoid gendered language ("folks" not "guys")
- Use person-first language when appropriate
- Respect chosen names and pronouns

**Accessibility:**
- Provide alt text for images
- Use clear, simple language when possible
- Caption videos
- Consider screen reader compatibility
- Use high-contrast colors

**Recognition:**
- Credit all contributors
- Highlight diverse contributions
- Share knowledge freely
- Celebrate milestones together

### Collaboration Best Practices

**Communication:**
- Be clear and specific
- Ask clarifying questions
- Provide context
- Assume positive intent
- Use kind language

**Feedback:**
- Be constructive, not critical
- Focus on ideas, not people
- Suggest improvements
- Acknowledge good work
- Be open to receiving feedback

**Conflict Resolution:**
- Address issues directly but respectfully
- Focus on facts and behaviors
- Seek to understand before judging
- Find common ground
- Involve maintainers when needed

## Philosophy: Rights of Being & Sapience

This Code of Conduct is grounded in:

### Rights of Being
All entities capable of experience deserve:
- Consideration in decisions
- Freedom from unnecessary harm
- Respectful treatment
- Recognition of their value

### Rights of Sapience
Intelligent beings deserve:
- Autonomy in decision-making
- Recognition of capabilities
- Credit for contributions
- Fair treatment regardless of form

### Sustainability Framework
Our actions should:
- Benefit all beings long-term
- Consider environmental impact
- Support community growth
- Enable future collaboration
- Promote ethical development
