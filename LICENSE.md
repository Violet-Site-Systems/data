# License Information

## Current Status

This project is currently being prepared for open-source release. A license will be selected from the **BGI Sustainability Codes Licenses** as specified in the project requirements.

## Pending License Selection

The project maintainers are evaluating the following licensing options to best align with the project's values of:
- **Sustainability:** Long-term benefit for all beings
- **Rights of Being:** Consideration for all entities capable of experience
- **Rights of Sapience:** Autonomy and respect for intelligent beings
- **Open Education:** Free access to knowledge
- **Ethical Development:** Transparent, responsible AI advancement

## Recommended Options

While the final decision is pending, the following licenses are under consideration:

### Option 1: BGI Sustainability Codes License (Preferred)
The project aims to use a license from the BGI Sustainability Codes framework, which ensures:
- Sustainability requirements for derivative works
- Fair use provisions
- Protection of commons
- Long-term thinking and impact consideration
- Alignment with Rights of Being and Sapience principles

**Reference:** BGI Sustainability Codes Licenses (to be specified)

### Option 2: Dual Licensing
Alternatively, a dual licensing approach may be adopted:

**For Code:**
- **GPL-3.0** (Strong copyleft, ensures derivatives stay open)
- **OR Apache-2.0** (Permissive, allows commercial use with attribution)

**For Data:**
- **CC-BY-SA-4.0** (Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike)
  - Allows: Sharing, adapting, commercial use
  - Requires: Attribution, ShareAlike

**For Documentation:**
- **CC-BY-SA-4.0** (Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike)
