Theory Overview: In Fred Gangstad's "Mother Rock" cosmology, galaxies form from a central dense core ejecting matter through Z-pinch plasma tubes. Initial straight polar jets create a galactic bar structure. A 90° gyroscopic shift of the core causes the jets to trail, curving into spiral arms. Stars condense in the cooling plasma wakes left behind, forming the luminous spiral structure we observe.

Note: This is an artistic visualization of an alternative cosmological model and is not mainstream scientific consensus.
